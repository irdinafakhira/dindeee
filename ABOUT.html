DINDE

Japanese Restaurant

Welcome to Dinde, an authentic Japanese dining experience located in the heart of Terengganu.

Our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the finest flavors of Japan, with a focus on traditional

dishes, fresh ingredients, and warm hospitality. Whether you're a long-time lover of Japanese cuisine

or new to its offerings, Dinde promises to deliver a memorable dining experience that caters to all your

senses. At Dinde, we believe in the beauty of simplicity. Inspired by the art of Japanese cooking,

we meticulously craft each dish to ensure it embodies both authenticity and innovation. From our signature

sushi rolls to flavorful ramen bowls, each item on our menu is made with the highest quality ingredients,

carefully selected to reflect the true essence of Japanese culinary traditions. Our restaurant is designed

to immerse you in the serene ambiance of Japan. With our minimalist decor, soothing colors, and attentive

service, we aim to create a peaceful and welcoming atmosphere for all our guests. Whether you're dining with

family, friends, or colleagues, Dinde is the perfect place to enjoy a taste of Japan in Terengganu. Come

experience the culture, flavor, and tradition of Japan at Dinde where every meal is an opportunity to celebrate

the art of Japanese cuisine

Vision

ビジョン

To be the top choice for authentic Japanese cuisine in Kuala Terengganu, offering a unique and enjoyable dining experience that brings the true taste of Japan to the local community.

Mission

ミッション

  • To serve high-quality, authentic Japanese dishes with fresh ingredients.
  • To create a warm, welcoming atmosphere for all customers.
  • To provide excellent service and a memorable dining experience.
  • To introduce the flavors of Japan to the local community.

Objectives

目的

  • To provide authentic and high-quality Japanese cuisine to the local community and visitors in Kuala Terengganu.
  • To offer a diverse menu that showcases traditional Japanese dishes, including sushi, ramen, don, and shabu shabu, catering to different tastes and preferences.
  • To create a warm, inviting atmosphere that encourages guests to enjoy a relaxed and memorable dining experience.
  • To maintain excellent customer service, ensuring each guest feels valued and satisfied with their meal.
